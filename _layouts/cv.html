🎀 I am skeptical of AI progress that scales everything except accountability. 🎀

My research sits at the intersection of three questions: how do we adapt powerful models without requiring unlimited resources? How do we evaluate them honestly, beyond what aggregate metrics are designed to hide? And who are we building them for? These questions have taken me from parameter-efficient fine-tuning to adversarial benchmarking to low-resource NLP — and increasingly toward mechanistic interpretability, where the goal is understanding not just what models output, but what they actually represent.

Efficiency, interpretability, and inclusion are not separate research agendas for me. They are the same argument at different levels of abstraction.
